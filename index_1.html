Our Story

From a single machine to a full-service precision facility.

E3 Tool was founded on a bedrock of over 20 years of hands-on manufacturing and machining experience. After two decades on the shop floor mastering programming, complex setups, and precision cutting, we built this independent shop to bring veteran technical insight directly to our clients' projects.

We operate as a lean, highly focused CNC machine shop equipped to handle complex components, rapid prototyping, and short-run production without the heavy corporate overhead. Because we maintain direct oversight of every project, every single part that leaves our bench undergoes rigorous inspection to ensure it matches your blueprints exactly. We are committed to growing our business the right way—by delivering honest lead times, sharp attention to detail, and precision manufacturing you can rely on.
